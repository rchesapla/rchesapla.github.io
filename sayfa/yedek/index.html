Data Entry

To perform the calculation, first copy all the coins as shown in the image, paste them into the Data Entry section, and click the Convert to Table button. Then, enter your Power and click the Calculate button. For example, if your power is 22.287 EH, write 22287, if your power is 950 PH, write 950.
